Privacy Policy

GreenPlatter ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data:

  • Personal Data: When you register for an account, we collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, and other contact details.
  • Health Data: To provide personalized meal plans, we may collect sensitive health-related information, such as your blood glucose levels, dietary preferences, and other medical history that you voluntarily provide.
  • Device Data: We may collect information about your device, including your IP address, operating system, and usage patterns to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and deliver personalized meal plans based on your health and dietary data.
  • Improve and optimize the App through technical and usage data.
  • Communicate with you about app updates, changes, or service-related information.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will not share your personal or health data with third parties unless:

  • It is required by law.
  • We have your explicit consent.
  • We need to collaborate with trusted partners to deliver essential features of the App (e.g., data storage).

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. However, no online service is completely secure. By using GreenPlatter, you acknowledge that any transmission of personal data is done at your own risk.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You may also request that we stop processing your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@greenplatter.net.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App, and continued use of the App after such changes indicates your acceptance of the new terms.

For further inquiries or requests, please contact us at support@greenplatter.net.
